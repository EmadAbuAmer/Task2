Tubas

Tubas is a Palestinian city located in the northeastern West Bank, part of the Tubas Governorate.
It is situated around 21 kilometers northeast of Nablus and close to the Jordan Valley, making it part of the wider Jordan Rift Valley.
The city's population is predominantly Palestinian, and it serves as an agricultural hub due to its fertile lands.
